## DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Color Palette:
- **Primary**: #22c55e (R-Tech green)
- **Accent**: #10b981 (Darker green)
- **Text**: #1f2937 (Dark gray)
- **Background**: #ffffff (White) or #f9fafb (Light gray)
- **Problem/Warning**: #ef4444 (Red)
- **Success**: #22c55e (Green)

### Fonts:
- **Headlines**: Poppins Bold or Montserrat Bold
- **Body**: Inter Regular or Poppins Regular
- **Size**: Headlines 32-48pt, Body 14-18pt

### Icons:
Use from Lucide Icons or Heroicons (consistent with website)

### Layout:
- Clean, minimal design
- Lots of white space
- One main message per slide
- Use icons/graphics (not text-heavy)

### Consistency:
- Use same header/footer across all slides
- Consistent icon style
- Slide numbers bottom right

---

## EXPORT SETTINGS (Canva/PowerPoint)

### For Printing:
- PDF format
- High quality (300 DPI)
- Include bleed (if printing)

### For Screen Presentation:
- PDF or PowerPoint
- 1920x1080 resolution (16:9 aspect ratio)
- Embed fonts

### For Leave-Behind:
- PDF format
- Compressed (< 5MB for easy WhatsApp sharing)
- Include clickable links (QR codes as backup)

---

## USAGE TIPS

1. **Practice with Slides**: Rehearse 3x minimum
2. **Time Each Slide**: Aim for 30-60 seconds per slide
3. **Total Presentation**: 10-15 minutes (leave time for Q&A)
4. **Print Backup**: Bring printed version (if projector fails)
5. **Mobile-Ready**: Have PDF on your phone (show on owner's phone)
